Introduction

In German language, there are three definite articles for nouns in singular: der for masculine nouns, die for feminine nouns and das for neutral nouns. German native speakers know mostly intuitively what the article of each noun is. However, non-native speakers need to memorize the articles. There are several rules and guidelines determining the articles of some categories of nouns. But beware exceptions.


 
 

1. Rules for Article 'Der'

The following nouns have the article der:

Names Examples
Nouns for masculine persons and functions/professions Vater, Pilot, Arzt
Seasons Frühling, Sommer, Herbst, Winter
Months Januar, Juli, Dezember
Days of the week Montag, Dienstag, Sonntag
Compass directions Nordwest(en), Süd(en)
Precipitations Regen, Schnee, Hagel
Car brands Audi, BMW, Mercedes
Trains IC
Nouns derived from verbs without suffix Gang, Fang

Furthermore, nouns with the suffixes below have the article der:

Suffix Examples
-er (nouns derived from verbs) Fahrer, Lehrer
-ismus Kapitalismus, Journalismus

The following categories of nouns have mainly the article der, but beware exceptions:

Names Examples Exceptions
Alcoholic beverages Cognac, Wein, Whiskey das Bier
Rivers outside Europe Amazonas, Mississippi
Mountains Mont Blanc, Kilimanjaro die Zugspitze

Most of nouns with the following suffixes have the article der, but beware exceptions:

Suffix Examples Exceptions
-ant Demonstrant, Elefant das Croissant, das Restaurant
-ling Lehrling, Schützling das Dribbling, das Bowling
-ner Rentner, Schaffner, Zöllner das Banner, die Wiener (Wurst)
-or Motor, Traktor das Gegentor, das Chlor

Beware: This is applicable only to nouns in singular. All nouns in plural have the article die.

Beware: Diminutives have always the article das: der Kopf → das Köpfchen.


 
 

2. Rules for Article 'Die'

The following nouns have the article die:

Names Examples
Nouns for feminine persons and functions/professions Mutter, Friseuse, Ärztin
Motorcycle brands Harley Davidson, Yamaha, BMW (only motorcycle)
Planes and ships Boeing 747, Titanic
Cardinal numbers Eins, Drei

The following categories of nouns have mainly the article die, but beware exceptions:

Names Examples Exceptions
Plants and trees Birke, Chrysantheme, Rose der Ahorn, das Veilchen

Furthermore, nouns with the suffixes below have the article die:

Suffix Examples
-falt Vielfalt
-heit Freiheit, Sicherheit
-keit Möglichkeit, Schnelligkeit
-schaft Freundschaft, Mannschaft
-t (nouns derived from verbs) Fahrt, Tat
-ung Leitung, Zeitung

Foreign nouns with the suffixes below have the article die:

Suffix Examples
-ade Hitparade, Marmelade
-age Garage, Passage
-anz Eleganz, Dominanz
-enz (nouns derived from verbs) Existenz, Tendenz
-ik Kritik, Musik
-ion Diskussion, Koalition
-tät Identität, Qualität
-ur Agentur, Reparatur

Most of nouns with the following suffixes have the article die, but beware exceptions:

Suffix Examples Exceptions
-e Grenze, Lampe der Junge, der Friede
-ei Abtei, Metzgerei das Ei, der Papagei
-ie Diplomatie, Psychologie der Junkie, der Hippie
-in Ärztin, Studentin das Benzin, der Harlekin

Beware: Diminutives have always the article das: die Hand → das Händchen.


 
 

3. Rules for Article 'Das'

The following nouns have the article das:

Names Examples
Diminutives (-chen, -lein) Kaninchen, Fräulein
Nouns derived from infinitives Essen, Schreiben
Nouns derived from adjectives Gute, Böse
Colours Rot, Gelb, Blau

The following categories of nouns have mainly the article das, but beware exceptions:

Names Examples Exceptions
Almost all of the 112 known chemical elements Aluminium, Kupfer, Uran 6 Exceptions:
der Kohlenstoff, der Sauerstoff, der Stickstoff, der Wasserstoff, der Phosphor, der Schwefel
Metals Blei, Messing, Zinn die Bronze, der Stahl
Fractions Drittel (⅓), Viertel (¼) die Hälfte (½)

Furthermore, nouns with the suffixes below have the article das:

Suffix Examples
-ial Material, Potenzial

Most of nouns with the following suffixes have the article das, but beware exceptions:

Suffix Examples Exceptions
-ment Instrument, Parlament der Konsument, der Zement
-nis Ergebnis, Tennis die Fahrerlaubnis, die Wildnis
-o Auto, Konto die Avocado, der Euro
-tum Quantum, Ultimatum der Reichtum, der Irrtum
-um (nouns of Latin origin) Publikum, Museum, Stadium

Beware: This is applicable only to nouns in singular. All nouns in plural have the article die.


 
 

4. Words with more Articles

There are also many nouns with more articles. In some cases, the article determines the meaning of the word:

Der Die Das
der Band (hardcover book) die Band (music group) das Band (ribbon, tape)
der Lama (buddhist priest) das Lama (animal)
der Kiwi (bird) die Kiwi (fruit)

Sometimes, two articles can be used:

  • der / das Logo
  • der / das Techno
  • der / das Virus
